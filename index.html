Problem

Operator X in the Permian Basin was applying an incorrect fuel and compression deduction rate based on mineral lease provisions or outdated JDA amendments for Non-op WI.

  • Initial Loss Rate: ~1.1% of gross revenue being over-deducted monthly.
  • Missing Revenue Identified: With using only 3 of 6 algorithms, S.IS uncovered missing revenues of 8.5% with remaining algorithms expected to add an additional 5.75% covering the period from Jan-25 through Jul-25.
